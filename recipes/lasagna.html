World's Best Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna Lush Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Italian Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Pasta Sheets
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and prepare ingredients
  2. Cook the meats and make the meat sauce
  3. Cook the pasta sheets
  4. Make the ricotta mixture
  5. Layer the lasagna in a ceramic dish
  6. Cover with foil and place in oven
  7. Let the lasagna rest then serve
